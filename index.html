Farm-to-market supply chain traceability

Dandelion is a friendly application to help make compliance with FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA) convenient and valuable.

Continue

Dandelion experts turn open source technology into preventative traceability measures the way you need it.

Millions of supply chain trace records have been captured by thousands using applications Dandelion principals have designed. Get started now on your own smartphone with the Dandelion Android App or explore some of our favorites.

Let us know if you'd like us to proudly showcase your inspiring traceability projects built with Dandelion.

Explore the Technology

Designed to make traceability simple for everyone, everywhere.

Dandelion makes front-end traceability faster and easier. It's made for folks of all skill levels comfortable with modern smartphone devices, and supply chains of all sizes.

Sass less software

dandelion smartphone app is free, our software captures your traceabilty and defaults to private, you selectively decide to share your traceability or not to, based on your market needs and supply chain risks. Software as simple service, less to install. Quickly get started with Google Play Android App.

One solution, many devices.

dandelion easily and efficiently scales your traceability with a single code base, from phones to tablets to desktops to cloud website with picture based queries.

Full of features

With Dandelion, you get critial compliance records by taking beautiful pictures of your produce, to make your produce more valuable.

Dandelion is open source. It's hosted, developed, and maintained on GitHub. Holds numerous compelling advantages for businesses.
